Handicap Committee

The Handicap Committee sits the night before the first round. As most competitors do not have official handicaps, each player proposes their desired handicap. The group votes, and final handicaps are set for Day 1. From there, a realignment system is used.

Handicap Realignment

After each round, Stableford scores are reviewed. Competitors' handicaps are adjusted according to the following system:

ConditionAdjustment
Maximum Handicap Increase8
Each point over 36-1 handicap
Exactly 36 pointsNo change
Every 2 points under 36+1 handicap
Maximum Handicap Decrease4

Stableford Competition

Points are awarded based on net score (adjusted for handicap) for each hole as follows:

ScorePoints
Par2
Birdie (1 under)3
Eagle (2 under)4
Bogey (1 over)1
Double Bogey or worse0

The player with the highest total after 18 holes is the winner. Handicaps are capped at 28 for men. If there is a tie after all rounds, the Loons Masters Invitational ends in a draw!
