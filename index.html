🏌️‍♂️ Welcome to the Loons Masters Invitational 🍻

It all kicked off back in 2001 — a handful of Loons rented a couple of Hunter’s Lodges, Riverside Park in Forfar. We partied hard Friday (unless a migraine sent you to bed early). Then hacked/drank our way through a round of golf on Saturday and another on Sunday. It was messy, it was brilliant — and we knew we had to do it again.

So the following year the Loons Master Invitational was formed!!!

Once a year, a bunch of Loons, golf-bandits, drinking warriors descend upon some unsuspecting course for what can only be described as... a beautiful alcohol-fueled disaster.

The Loons Masters isn’t just a golf tournament — it’s a catch-up of great/old mates:

  • 🍺 Questionable drinking decisions
  • 🏌️‍♂️ Even worse swings
  • 🎁 Prizes no one wants
  • 🤝 Banter and golf tamper like nothing else

Since 2001, this annual long weekend of chaos has been our tradition — come rain, shine, or sideways sleet — to catch up, trade tall tales, and attempt (often badly) to play 2 or 3 rounds of golf while clinging to our dignity and our pints.

Whether you’re here for the glory, the laughs, or just to see who’s still standing by Sunday night — welcome back. The fairways await, the banter’s flowing, and the Loons are very much in session.
